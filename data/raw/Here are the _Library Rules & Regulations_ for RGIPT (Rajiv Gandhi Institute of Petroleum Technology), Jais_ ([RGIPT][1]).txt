Here are the Library Rules & Regulations for RGIPT (Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Petroleum Technology), Jais: (RGIPT)


Library Rules & Regulations (Summary)

Opening Hours

9:00 AM – 11:00 PM, daily (RGIPT)
During exam days: 9:00 AM to 2:00 AM (five days before mid/end semester exams) (RGIPT)
Closed on gazetted holidays (RGIPT)

Circulation Hours

Monday to Saturday: 11:00 AM – 8:00 PM (RGIPT)
Sunday: No circulation (RGIPT)

Discipline & General Rules

Silence must be observed at all times in library and its environs. (RGIPT)
Use of mobile phones is prohibited. (RGIPT)
Bags, cases, folders etc. must be left outside in the luggage area. (RGIPT)
No overnight baggage storage in the library. (RGIPT)
Users must show all items before leaving the library. (RGIPT)
No student allowed to enter the circulation area (unsupervised?). (RGIPT)
Defacing / marking / mutilation of books is disallowed. Users will be held responsible. (RGIPT)
Loss of library card must be reported immediately. (RGIPT)
No eating, drinking, smoking or sleeping in the library. (RGIPT)

Borrowing / Issue Privileges

Member Type Number of Books Duration
Faculty 20 One year (RGIPT)
Staff 4 Two months (RGIPT)
PhD students 4 One month (RGIPT)
Post-Graduate students (M.Tech / MBA) 2 One month (RGIPT)
Under-Graduate students 2 20 days (RGIPT)
Book Bank (UG/PG) 2 One semester (RGIPT)

Reference books, newspapers, journals & magazines cannot be taken out; these are for in-library use only. (RGIPT)
All users must present their library card when borrowing. (RGIPT)
Borrowed items must be returned by due date; overdue fines apply. (RGIPT)
Students with overdue materials or fines are disallowed from further library services until the dues are cleared. (RGIPT)

Fines / Loss / Damage

Overdue fine: ₹ 3 per day for general collection books & book‐bank issues. (RGIPT)
Lost book or damage: The user is responsible; replacement or payment is required. (RGIPT)
If book is out of print, double the MRP value may be charged. (RGIPT)
Lost books must be replaced or paid within 30 days. (RGIPT)
