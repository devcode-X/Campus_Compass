RGIPT Jais Hostel Rules & Regulations — Detailed

General Principles & Accommodation

RGIPT is fully-residential and all registered students must stay in hostels. 
Rooms are shared by two students, with furniture provided: beds, study tables, chairs, almirahs, ceiling fans, tube lights, electrical sockets, etc. 
Room allotment (which room, which furniture) is under the discretion of the institute/warden.

Discipline & Prohibitions

Ragging is strictly prohibited — it’s a cognizable offense. 
Smoking, consumption of alcohol, intoxicants, or drugs inside or outside hostel premises is forbidden. Violation may lead to rustication from hostel or the whole institute. 
Use of certain electrical appliances in rooms is not allowed: heaters, coolers, iron, induction cook-tops, heating rods etc.

Timings, Entry & Permissions

Students must return to campus by 08:00 PM daily. 
If one needs to stay out later for any valid reason, written prior permission from the warden is required. 
Every student should record entry/exit at the Institute Gate.

Maintenance & Upkeep

Students are responsible collectively & individually for maintaining hostel property. Any damage to room or its fixtures/furniture must be borne by the residents. 
Deliberate destruction leads to penalties that may include fines or even expulsion from hostel. 
Conservation of electricity: Students must switch off lights, fans when leaving rooms. 
Decorations like posters must not damage the walls; no posters put up without permission; spoiling walls/property is prohibited.

Mess & Fees

Mess is compulsory for all hostel residents. 
Hostel & messing charges must be paid on time.
