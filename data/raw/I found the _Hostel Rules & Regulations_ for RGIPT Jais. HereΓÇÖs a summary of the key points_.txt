I found the Hostel Rules & Regulations for RGIPT Jais. Here’s a summary of the key points:


RGIPT Jais — Hostel Rules & Regulations

(Source: Hostel Rules PDF by Hostel Office) (RGIPT)

RGIPT is a fully residential institute; all registered students are required to stay in hostels. (RGIPT)


Rooms are shared (two students per room) and equipped with furniture like bed, study tables, chair, almirah, fans & lights. (RGIPT)


Ragging is strictly prohibited; it’s a cognizable offense. (RGIPT)


Mess is compulsory for all hostel residents & mess fees must be paid timely. (RGIPT)


Use of intoxicants (alcohol, drugs), smoking is strictly prohibited inside or outside hostel premises; violation may lead to rustication from hostel or institute. (RGIPT)


Student Timings

All students must return to campus by 8:00 PM daily. (RGIPT)
If a student needs to stay out later for special reasons, prior written permission from the warden is needed. (RGIPT)
Entry / exit must be recorded at institute gate. (RGIPT)


Electrical appliances usage is regulated: items like heaters, coolers, irons, induction cooktops etc. are not permitted. (RGIPT)


Maintenance / Upkeep

Damage or loss of hostel property/furniture is the financial responsibility of the residents. (RGIPT)
Students are expected to keep their rooms clean. (RGIPT)
Lights/fans should be switched off when leaving rooms to conserve electricity. (RGIPT)
Hostel authorities have the right to enter and inspect rooms anytime. (RGIPT)
